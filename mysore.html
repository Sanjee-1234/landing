MYSORE

Mysore (officially Mysuru) is a major city in Karnataka, known for its royal heritage, intricate architecture, and vibrant
culture. Situated at the base of the Chamundi Hills, it was the capital of the Kingdom of Mysore for nearly six centuries,
with the Wadiyar dynasty as its main patrons of art and culture. It is often called the "City of Palaces"
