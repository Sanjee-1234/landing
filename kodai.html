KODAIKANAL

Kodaikanal, known as the "Princess of Hill Stations," is a picturesque town in the Dindigul district of Tamil Nadu. Situated
at an elevation of about 2,133 meters in the Palani Hills of the Western Ghats, the town was established in 1845 as a summer
retreat for American missionaries and British bureaucrats to escape the heat of the plains.
